TT Odyssey

I completed a year long internship at TT Odyssey, developers of LEGO Star Wars Battles. I spent the first 9 months working with the engineering team, and an additional 3 months working with the design team.

In the middle of my internship, I was tasked with updating a project to a significantly newer version of Unity, which required working closely with QA. It gave me very valuable experience with using XCode and Jenkins.

I worked for several months on gameplay and combat prototyping.

During my time at Odyssey I completed a 3 day first aid training course, which gave me widely applicable first aid skills.

For the last 3 months of my time at Odyssey, I worked as a junior designer integrated fully into the design team.
