AI Pond Life Simulation

Using Unity, Two different AI agents are simulated - a Utility AI (with wants and needs like The Sims), and a behavior tree AI.

The prey agents eat the green food, and the predator agents eat the prey.

The prey agents birth live young whereas the predator agents lay eggs which the prey can eat.

Prey agents exhibit emergent flocking behavior, and both types of agent pass down genes, which can mutate, to their young.
